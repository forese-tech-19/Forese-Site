Mocks
The Mock Placements is an annual preparatory event held exclusively for the pre-final year students. It has been the flagship event of FORESE for the past twenty years. Over a hundred recruiters, from different spheres of the industry, come in to interview the candidates enrolled for this event. The Mocks is preceded by two events: an Aptitude Test and a Group Discussion. With their Aptitude and Group Discussion scores in-hand, the candidates are set to have a one-to-one interview with the companies they wish to be recruited into. The event gives students an insight on requirements of the industry, identify areas of potential improvement and, rectify their shortcomings. The recruiters get a chance to identify skilled individuals whom they can recruit during placements and offer internship opportunities to deserving candidates. The event also provides a platform for them to contribute to the educational community by furnishing students with their strengths and weaknesses and skill set requirement, streamlining their preparation for the actual placements. To sum up, MOCK offers a one-of-a-kind platform which gives students a much-needed confidence boost to face future placements and claim their dream job.

GroupDiscission
The Group Discussions offer a first-hand experience of recruitments, through which the student’s communicative skills are assessed. Each student attends a round of GD, through which they identify areas of potential improvement pertaining to general knowledge, debate and presentation skills. The students are evaluated based on their ability to put forth their views and their receptiveness to other’s opinions. The marks, along with those of the Aptitude Assessment Test, are reviewed by the recruiters on the day of Mock Placements. To the recruiters, the Group Discussions act as a simulated managerial environment. It helps them in identifying individuals with proficient leadership and interpersonal skills. The event provides a platform to test the entrant’s analytical prowess and their ability to think on their feet.

Apptitude Test
The Aptitude Assessment Test is an online test in which the candidate’s dexterity and cognitive skills are assessed. The students are questioned on their English and Technical knowledge, where a different set of questions are allotted for each department. This helps the participants identify fields of study which need to be tweaked. It also throws light on how aptitude tests would take place during campus placements. The marks scored, along with the Group Discussion scores, are put up for evaluation to the recruiters in the Mock Placements. On the other hand, this enables a standardized method for the recruiters to evaluate and score a candidate’s skills, with quantified results for comparison against other applicants. The event delivers an opportunity for students to experience an aptitude test and develop their problem solving abilities.

ForED
ForEd is a biannual education fair organized for students who aspire to study abroad. The event, organized for final and pre final years, aims to expose them to the plethora of opportunities available across the world. Through representation of universities across the world, they acquire a global perspective, comparing and contrasting various courses. With the help of experienced counsellors, students are able to zero in on their ideal destination. After an interaction with the counsellors, the candidates then proceed to interact with university representatives from countries such as the USA, UK, Australia and Canada. They gain a more specific insight on the various aspects like tuition, prerequisites and deadlines of the programmes available. Various banks are also in attendance for the event, offering guidance on the various loan schemes available. The event is instrumental in shaping the minds of students, guiding them and giving them a clear picture of the foreign education scene.
